Erasmus+ projects

I Successfully took a part in an Erasmus+ Youth Exchange project named ,,CUTE'' in Hungary where the overall aim and specific objectives achieved from this project were: To highlight shared values, initiate tolerance and acceptance for other cultures, to empower young people to realize needs, plan and take actions. I also took part in another project named ,,Natural Acceptance", which took place in Hungary. As a result of our project I improved the level of key competences of myself, increased solidarity and acceptance and enhanced self-expression. Photograph

Capturing moments through the lens is both an art and a passion that defines my photography. With an eye for detail and a keen sense of composition, I strive to immortalize the beauty and essence of the world around me. Each click of the shutter is a unique opportunity to freeze time, telling a story through the interplay of light, color, and emotion. From breathtaking landscapes to intimate portraits, my goal is to evoke a sense of wonder and connection in those who view my photographs.
